# 🛒 E-commerce Customer Behaviour Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing **customer behaviour in an e-commerce environment** to uncover patterns, trends, and actionable business insights. The goal is to understand how customers interact with products and what factors influence their purchasing decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer purchasing patterns
* Identify key factors affecting buying behaviour
* Perform customer segmentation
* Generate insights for business decision-making
* Improve customer retention and marketing strategies

---

## 📊 Dataset

* Source: E-commerce dataset
* Contains:

  * Customer details (Age, Gender, Location)
  * Transaction data
  * Product information
  * Purchase frequency and spending

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

* **Python**
* **Pandas & NumPy**
* **Matplotlib & Seaborn**
* **Jupyter Notebook**
* **Excel / CSV Data**
* (Optional: Power BI / SQL if used)

---

## 🔍 Analysis Performed

* Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
* Customer Segmentation
* Purchase Behaviour Analysis
* Trend & Pattern Identification

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Identified high-value customers based on spending
* Found most popular product categories
* Analyzed customer purchase frequency
* Discovered trends based on demographics
* Highlighted opportunities for targeted marketing
